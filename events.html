One Week March Break Workshop for kids aged 6-13.

Join us at ChorusMusic for a full day of financial literacy fun!

Students will learn about topics like bank accounts, piggy bank savings, budgeting, and an intro to credit & investments by participating in a variety of interactive activities and games such as:

  • Life Simulator: Pick a career, complete tasks to earn in-game money, and choose how you will spend it.
  • Budgeting Blitz: Put together a personal and household budget. How would it change if something in your life changed?
  • WalletWiz Monopoly: Win prizes playing Monopoly with an interesting twist. And more!
  • Plus - Musical breaks throughout the day with music games and activities including musical fitness, drum circles and more!
