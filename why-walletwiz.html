Zero-Based Approach

At WalletWiz, we take a zero-based approach to financial literacy education. This means that our beginner courses start…well…right from the beginning. Most people who aren’t exposed to the world of finance through work, or other personal experience typically have little to no knowledge about Canadian personal finance systems, and this is why our courses start from scratch. You do not need to have any prior knowledge to begin your journey with us, but as you go through it you will find yourself closer and closer to achieving financial wellness.

One Stop Shop

We are your one stop shop for financial literacy! We know that everyone has a different learning style, which is why we offer a comprehensive suite of solutions for you to choose from. Whether you prefer an in-person workshop for your class or group, or using our convenient 24/7 online training facility for self-study, we are sure you will find a solution that meets your needs. Our online portal allows you to take a variety of courses and access additional material so you can continue your financial wellness journey even once you've completed an in-person workshop.
