Raj Raman

Raj Raman is a passionate educator, speaker and entrepreneur. At the age of 18, he was hired by a major Canadian bank as one of their youngest advisors. While working at the bank, Raj realized that many of his clients had gaps in their financial knowledge - which prevented them from being able to advance in their lives. This is where he identified that there is a significant need for financial literacy education in Canada.
